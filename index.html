GIS & Utility Network Projects

➤ This page presents a collection of GIS projects focused on infrastructureand utility network mapping. The work includes spatial analysis,data management, and digital mapping using professional geospatial tools.

  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS) is considered one of the most important modern applied sciences that has brought a transformative shift in how we understand and analyze the world around us. It integrates geography, computer science, data analysis, and engineering to provide a comprehensive system for managing and analyzing spatial data and linking it with attribute data to support decision-making.

  • GIS is no longer merely a tool for map creation; it has evolved into an advanced analytical platform used in urban planning, infrastructure and utility network management, transportation, energy, environmental management, defense, asset management, and many other vital sectors.
